Bread and Cup

Bread & Cup is a local Lincoln, Nebraska restaurant that focuses purely on providing simple high quality food. This campaign focuses on communicating the restaurant’s simplicity. The components within the campaign included: three print ads, a roll up banner, and a video to be displayed on social media. The demographic for this campaign incuded local lincoln individuals ages, 35–50, who are looking for a sophisticated atomosphere and high quality food.
