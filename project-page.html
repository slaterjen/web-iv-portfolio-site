The BLACK MARKET

The Black Market is a trendy used clothing store located in Lincoln&#44; Nebraska. The client was in need of an updated logo to match the style of the clothing sold throughout the store. The design needed to attract a younger audience from 19-25&#44; who are looking for a fashionable&#44; edgy styled clothing for a cheaper price compared to mainstream retail store prices.

The overall design of this logo was to play off of the idea of an actual black market. With that in mind&#44; the design uses a bolder mark to help tie in the daring act of a black market&#44; combining the the colors black and yellow to suggest an audacious tone.
