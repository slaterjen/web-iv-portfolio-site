The BLACK MARKET

The Black Market is a trendy used clothing store located in Lincoln, Nebraska. The client was in need of an updated logo to match the style of the clothing sold throughout the store. The design needed to attract a younger audience from 19-25, who are looking for a fashionable edgy styled clothing for a cheaper price compared to mainstream retail store prices.

The overall design of this logo was to play off of the idea of an actual black market. With that in mind, the design uses a bolder mark to help tie in the daring act of a black market, combining the the colors black and yellow to suggest an audacious tone.
