Barrymore's Bar & Lounge

Barrymore’s is a classy bar and lounge located in Lincoln, Nebraska. The client was in need of an updated logo to compete with its competitors more accurately. The design needed to attract a younger audience from 25-30, who are looking for a classier bar with quality drinks.

Through the brainstorming process of this logo, the original idea of creating a geometric mark soon evolved to a new idea. The letter “B” in the mark was actually inspired by the blackletter style, but with a more contemporary twist. Taking a modern spin on the blackletter plays into the concept of the logo, by taking this route the mark combines a twist of old and new, trying in the characteristics of the bar itself.
