Smart Kart

Smart Kart is a mobile app for college students who shop at Hy-Vee. The app is paired up with the Hy-Vee to help make shopping with roommates easier. The app also helps split the bill and pay right from your home! Also featured, is the option to have your groceries delivered to your home.

The concept of this app was inpsired by my everyday life. In the brainstorming process of this concept, I knew I wanted an app that would make my life easier. As a college student, I was constantly keeping track of how much I was spending on groceries as I was shopping, while also trying to split grocery bills with my roommates. In the app you can shop right in your own home, and share the app with your roommates to help come up with a solid shopping list. When you are finished shopping, the app allows you to split the bill with your designated roommates and the option to either pick up your groceries or have them delivered to your home.
