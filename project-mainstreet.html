Main Street Coffee

Mainstreet Coffee is a handcrafted coffee shop located in Fairbury, Nebraska. The client was looking for a logo design to establish their new business in the small town. The design needed to attract a younger audience from 25–30, along with a secondary audience 30–45, who are locals within the small town looking for a high quality coffee beverage.

With the coffee shop actually being located on the mainstreet of the small town, the concept of the logo played into this. Implementing the crest design with the logo helped tie all of the elements together, with also providing a vintage characteristic.
