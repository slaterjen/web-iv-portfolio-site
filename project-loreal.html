Loreal

Loreal strives to provide high quality products for its consumers. The client needed an annual report to attract a younger female demographic. This audience included women, 25–30, who are fashionable women and pay attention to beauty trends.

The overall design of this annual report was inspired by fashion and beauty magazines, such as “Vogue” and “Allure”. These publications are most likely what the demographic for this project pay the most attention to.
